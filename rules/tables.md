## Core Reference Tables

### Difficulty Class (DC) — "by 5's" (p178)

| DC | Task Difficulty |
|---|---|
| 5 | Very Easy |
| 10 | Moderate |
| 15 | Hard |
| 20 | Very Hard |
| 25 | Extremely Hard |
| 30 | Near Impossible |

Most Checks asked for are DC 10, 15, or 20. Beating a DC by 5+ = a greater success; failing by 5+ = a worse failure. (Full guidance in `general-rules.md`.)

### Armour Examples (p170)

Choose an Armour Style when you pick/craft armour. Values are bonuses to Defence; Heavy Armour also gives Physical Damage Reduction (PDR) but costs Speed and gives DisADV on Agility Checks.

| Armour | Style | PD | AD | PDR | Speed | Agility Checks |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Light** | Defensive | +1 | +1 | — | — | — |
| Light | Deflecting | +2 | — | — | — | — |
| Light | Fortified | — | +2 | — | — | — |
| **Heavy** | Defensive | +1 | +1 | Half | −1 | DisADV |
| Heavy | Deflecting | +2 | — | Half | −1 | DisADV |
| Heavy | Fortified | — | +2 | Half | −1 | DisADV |
| Heavy | Highly Defensive | +2 | +2 | — | −1 | DisADV |

**Customised armour:** pick Light or Heavy, then add 2 points of Armour Properties (e.g. +1 PD, +1 AD, PDR/EDR for Heavy; Bulky −1 Speed and Rigid DisADV-Agility are −1-cost drawbacks). Full property list in `general-rules.md`.

> **Weapon Styles & Properties** (Axe→Bleed, Hammer→Knockback, etc.) and **Shields** are described in full in `general-rules.md`. Base weapon damage is 1, modified by Style, Properties (e.g. Heavy +1), and Combat Mastery. *(The Shield Table's numeric values were restored to `general-rules.md` on 2026-07-04 from the source PDF p170: Light — Buckler +1 PD/Grasp, Round +1 AD/Toss, Heater +1/+1; Heavy — Kite +1/+2, Tower +2/+2, both −1 Speed + DisADV Agility; customised shields = 2 property points, and only Heavy shields may take PD/AD Increase twice.)*
