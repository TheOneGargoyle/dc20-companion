    Self-contained: engine, full catalog and all six ledgers are baked in (regenerate with
tools/builder_build.py). The page tries a live fetch() of the sibling source files first
and falls back to the bake, so it runs from file:// or a server. Pick a character with
?char=<handle>, start fresh with ?new=<class>, or use the dropdown. In-progress
work is kept in this browser's local storage and offered for resume; you can also re-load your own exported
YAML (the engine re-validates anything loaded). Export PRESERVES the ledger's own YAML comments (header
provenance, inline notes, section markers) by re-anchoring them onto the re-serialised file; any comment whose
anchor was edited away is collected, marked, at the bottom for review. It round-trips cleanly through the engine.
