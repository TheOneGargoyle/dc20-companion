House Rules & Conventions

Our table's conventions & handy references, alongside the DC20 0.10.5 rules.

Always round up

Any fraction rounds up, always. Official in DC20, but the rulebook only states it inside an example (the standalone rule looks to have been dropped between versions), so we call it out explicitly.

Sense Magic (broader than RAW)

By the book, Sense Magic detects specific creature-types within 20 Spaces via a Spell Check. At our table it senses magic generally (creatures, items, and effects), with loose, GM-driven adjudication (sometimes via an Awareness check instead of a Spell Check).

Design-math rules of thumb

From the DC20 devs' behind-the-scenes design notes (relayed via Darryl), for judging spends & encounters.
1 MP = 2 SP = 2 AP.
~6 rounds/day (≈ 2 combats × 3 rounds).
1 Talent = 1 Class Feature = 4 Ancestry Points = 8 AP/day.
~65% to-hit between equal-level combatants (a +5 attack hits on 8+, Heavy 13+, crit 20).

Free rebuild on ruleset updates

Every new DC20 version, all PCs get a free full rebuild to realign with the new rules. Keep each character's identity stable; make the minimum tweaks needed. Maneuvers and spells change the most.

About this app
